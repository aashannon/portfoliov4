Aaron 'Chescher' Shannon

Creating user-centered designs that enhance the digital experience.


I'm a UX Interaction Developer with a passion for creating user-centered designs that enhance the digital experience. My goal is to bridge the gap between technology and users, ensuring that every interaction is intuitive and enjoyable.
I have a background in computer science and design, which allows me to approach problems from both a technical and creative perspective.
I believe that understanding the user's needs is key to creating effective solutions. My skills include user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. I am proficient in various design tools and have experience working in agile environments. My strengths lie in my ability to empathize with users and my attention to detail.
